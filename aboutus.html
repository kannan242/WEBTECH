Webflix

Video Streaming and broadband connections help users around the globe download and watch large video files from the comfort of their homes. For today’s audiences it’s all about immediacy and mobility, the content they are looking for must be just a click away to fit their needs. Now everything is possible. Maybe you want to watch an episode of your favourite show when you are traveling, or maybe each member of your family wants to watch something different in a separate room of the house.

Webflix provides a personalized subscription service that allows our members to access movies (“Webflix content”) streamed over the Internet to certain Internet-connected TVs, computers and other devices.
